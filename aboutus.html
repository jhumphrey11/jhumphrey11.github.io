About Us

Our Story

My name is Jakyi Humphrey, and I’m the CEO of Elo Cybersecurity. We began our journey as a small project in my mom's basement, powered by a vision to make cybersecurity education accessible to everyone. Alongside my co-founder Joe, we built Elo with a passion for helping people stay safe online—without charging them for essential knowledge and tools.

Our company was founded on resilience, innovation, and a commitment to digital safety. Through hard work and community support, Elo Cybersecurity continues to grow as a trusted platform for cybersecurity awareness and solutions.

Our Mission

We believe the internet should be a place of learning and opportunity, not fear. Our mission is to protect individuals—especially vulnerable users such as seniors—from the growing number of online threats. Through free educational resources, daily security updates, and user-friendly tools, we empower everyone to take charge of their digital safety.

Cybersecurity for Seniors

Technology is evolving rapidly, and it's easy to feel left behind. At Elo Cybersecurity, we are devoted to helping elderly individuals understand and navigate the online world safely. Our easy-to-use software is designed with seniors in mind, offering reliable protection from viruses, malware, phishing, and other cyber threats.

We host regular workshops and publish accessible guides to teach seniors the basics of cybersecurity—because safety should never be limited by age.

What We Offer

  • Free antivirus and antimalware tools
  • Educational resources for all skill levels
  • Consultations and support tailored for seniors
