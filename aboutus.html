About Us

Background Information

My name is Jakyi Humphrey. I am the CEO of Elo Cybersecurity. We started as a small company in my mom's basement. I also have a buddy named Joe who is a co-founder of Elo. Our goal in building this website was to educate as many people around the world about cybersecurity without people having to pay a price to receive this information. With hard work and dedication, Elo cybersecurity can remain today. With the help, we can keep achieving.

Protecting Users

Our goal is to be able to protect users from the harm of the internet. Despite the internet being a great place to learn and grow, it can have its negatives. Here at Elo Cybersecurity, we provide daily updated information on how to protect yourself from harm on the internet and how you can improve your cybersecurity knowledge. We also provide a free software that blocks things such as viruses, malware, and worms, etc.

Cybersecurity For Seniors

Here at Elo Cybersecurity, we are devoted to educating and helping elderly people advance their knowledge within cybersecurity and protect them from the harms of the internet. We provide an easy-to-use to use software to protect elderly people on the internet. Our main focus is to help the industry within cybersecurity. We understand that technology is growing by the day, and we want to at least provide a way to help keep people educated.
