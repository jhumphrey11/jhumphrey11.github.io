Why Cybersecurity Matters in 2025

Cybersecurity is more critical than ever. As technology continues to evolve, so do the threats that target our devices and personal information. Phishing attacks, ransomware, and identity theft are on the rise—especially targeting vulnerable populations like the elderly.

Top 3 Tips to Stay Safe Online

  1. Use Strong Passwords: Avoid using easy-to-guess information. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication: This adds an extra layer of protection even if your password is stolen.
  3. Update Your Software: Keep all your apps and operating systems updated to protect against the latest vulnerabilities.

Recognizing Phishing Emails

Phishing emails often look like they come from trusted sources. Watch for:

  • Spelling and grammar mistakes
  • Suspicious links or attachments
  • Urgent messages asking for personal information
Never click on links unless you’re sure they’re safe.
