Educational Information

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from unauthorized access, theft, or damage. It’s essential in today’s digital world where threats like hacking, phishing, and malware are common. Basic steps include using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, keeping software updated, being cautious of suspicious emails, and regularly backing up data. Tools like firewalls and antivirus software also help prevent attacks. Whether you're an individual or a business, cybersecurity is vital to keeping your information safe online.
